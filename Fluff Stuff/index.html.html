The Fluff Stuff Story

Fluff Stuff was born from a simple yet profound realization: the most meaningful gifts are those that truly reflect the recipient's personality and the unique relationship you share with them.

In 2023, our founder Maya was searching for the perfect gift for her mother's milestone birthday. After hours of browsing generic pre-made gift baskets, she realized none of them captured her mother's vibrant personality or favorite things.

That night, she created a completely personalized hamper filled with her mother's favorite teas, books, and comfort items. When her mother burst into tears of joy upon receiving this thoughtfully curated gift, Maya knew she had discovered something special.

Within months, Fluff Stuff was launched with a mission to bring that same joy to gift-giving across India, transforming ordinary moments into extraordinary memories through thoughtfully customized gift experiences.
